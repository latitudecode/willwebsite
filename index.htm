    • William Trowbridge

    Producer

    Will Trowbridge is a film producer based in Los Angeles. He studied film production and theory while in college before working at The Walt Disney Company. There, he expanded his technical knowledge of film production through his work with studio equipment, cameras, and 3D technology. He began his career in feature-length film production with "Safety Not Guaranteed," assisting director Colin Trevorrow in project management and oversight. Will currently works writing, directing and producing in Los Angeles. He is constantly looking for opportunities to grow as a filmmaker and collaborator. Story, performance and quality lie at the center of Trowbridge's efforts.
